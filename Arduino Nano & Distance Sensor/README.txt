ARDUINO NANO & DISTANCE SENSOR
WITH LED DIODE


*** Hardware Components Needed: ***

-Arduino Nano (and Micro USB)
-HC-SR04 Sensor
-RGB LED Diode
	*the one I used is common cathode,
	but you can also use common anode
-Breadboard
-10 jumper cables
-3 resistors (I used 2-100ohm and 1-220ohm)

*** Software needed: ***

-Arduino IDE
